Motor Vehicles in the Indian Market

Motorcycle
A two-wheeled vehicle commonly used in India for daily travel due to its good mileage and affordability.
Scooter
A lightweight two-wheeler with automatic transmission, very popular among city commuters.
Car
A four-wheeled motor vehicle used for personal transportation and available in many models.
Bus
A large vehicle designed to carry many passengers and commonly used for public transport.
Truck
A heavy motor vehicle used to transport goods over long distances across the country.
